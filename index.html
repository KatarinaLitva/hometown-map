Winnipeg, the Gateway to the West

Did you know...

Winnipeg, Manitoba is centrally located within Canada, and is considered to be the centre of North America.
For a city of its size, Winnipeg has a population of over 700,000, there are a number of sports teams including CFL Football and NHL Hockey.

The Winnipeg Blue Bombers, the CFL Football team, play at the Investor's Group Field at the University of Manitoba campus. The Winnipeg Jets play hockey at Bell MTS Place which is located in downtown Winnipeg.

  • Tickets for the Blue Bombers can be purchased at: Investor's Group Field
  • Tickets for the Jets can be purchased at: Bell MTS Place
